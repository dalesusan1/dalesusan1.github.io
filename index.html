Summary

Here we analyze the weather conditions with respect to the lattitude. We look at Max Temperature, Humidity, Cloudiness and Wind Speed. The idea is to determine if there is a correlation between lattitude and these four factors. Using dot plots, we plot the data points and regression analysis is done to determine the strength of the relationship between these factors and the lattitude.
